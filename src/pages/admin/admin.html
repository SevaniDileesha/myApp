Dr. Deshapriya S. Wijetunge


Director General-SUNFO, Federation of SUNFO Global Youth Forums


Sustained Regional, National and Provincial Civil Society-United Nations- Government engagement on a Post-2015 development framework.
(a) To create a civil society position on apost-2015 framework which will be used as a lobbying tool at national level.
(b) To create specific. concrete policy asks for national governments aiming at influencing their position on the post-2015 debate, and using these asks in an ongoing and concerted manner to to engage with an influence governments.
(c) To feed into the UNITED NATIONS consultative process at national and international level.
(d) To amplify the voices of people directly affected to poverty and injustice and their perspectives on a future global framework.
